Info

Sorting and Filter

You can sort and filter your cards using the options available on the left (or on top, on smaller screens) of the editor. This allows you to quickly find the cards you want to add to your deck.

Adding and Removing Cards

To add a card to your deck, simply click on it in the card list. To remove a card, click the same card again in the card list. You can also remove a card by pressing on it longer in the deck area. Custom cards can be added by clicking the "Import Custom Card" button, where you can upload a .cmscard file (which you can make using the Card Maker).

Variants

You can cycle through all the variants of the official cards by clicking on a card in the deck area.

Download

You can download an image of your deck by clicking the "Download Deck Image" button. You can also choose to have a transparent background or a solid color background for your deck image and you can choose how the cards are arranged in the deck image by selecting the grid size.
